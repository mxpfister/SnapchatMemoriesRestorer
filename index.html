Important System Requirements

💡 Why do you need this tool?

The official Snapchat export provides unfinished files: filters are separated from the image, and the recording date is missing. Without adjustment, your phone's file management is a mess. This tool automatically fixes everything:

  • ✨ Embed filters: Texts and stickers are permanently inserted into photos/videos.
  • 🗓️ Correct date: Original date & GPS are added. Your phone gallery sorts everything perfectly!
  • 🔐 100% Private: Processing runs entirely locally in your browser. Nothing is uploaded!
ℹ️

How do I get my Snapchat export?

In the Snapchat app go to your profile → Settings → My Data.
Be sure to select "Export your Memories" and "Export JSON files" at the bottom! Swipe through the entire time period on the calendar and submit the request.
